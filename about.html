About Us

At Santhi Multispeciality Hospital, we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to all, not something that drains your savings. Yes, we operate a hospital, but more importantly—we serve our people. Through Arogyasri, eligible patients receive government-funded treatment, ensuring they get the medical care they need without facing financial stress.

We don’t believe in suggesting unnecessary tests or treatments. Our priority is genuine patient care, not profits. With highly experienced doctors and a dedicated nursing team, we focus on helping you recover the right way, with care and compassion.

Because at the end of the day, good health isn’t a luxury—it’s a necessity. And we’re here to make sure you receive it.

Arogyasri – Quality Healthcare at No Cost

Santhi Multispeciality Hospital is proud to be an empaneled hospital under the Arogyasri scheme, ensuring free medical treatment for eligible patients. This initiative, launched by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, covers essential healthcare services and expensive treatments at no cost.

  • Covers surgeries, hospital stays, food and medicines
  • No upfront payment required
  • Accessible to BPL families with Arogyasri health cards

Contact us today to check your eligibility and avail the benefits of Arogyasri!
